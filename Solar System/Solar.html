The Sun :

* Size: Diameter of about 1.39 10^6 kilometers.
* Orbit: Orbits the center of the Milky Way galaxy at a distance of roughly 27,000 light-years.
* Age: Approximately 4.6 billion years.
* Rotation: Differential rotation; at the equator, it's about 25 Earth days, and at the poles, it's about 35 Earth days.
* Revolution: Approximately 225-250 million years (one galactic year).

Mercury:

* Size: Mean radius of about 2,440 kilometers.
* Orbit: Average orbital radius of about 57.9 10^6 kilometers (0.39 AU).
* Age: Approximately 4.5 billion years (formed with the solar system).
* Rotation: About 59 Earth days.
* Revolution: About 88 Earth days.

Venus:

* Size: Mean radius of about 6,052 kilometers.
* Orbit: Average orbital radius of about 108.2 10^6 kilometers (0.72 AU).
* Age: Approximately 4.5 billion years.
* Rotation: About 243 Earth days (retrograde - spins backward).
* Revolution: About 225 Earth days.

Earth:

* Size: Mean radius of about 6,371 kilometers.
* Orbit: Average orbital radius of about 149.6 10^6 kilometers (1 AU).
* Age: Approximately 4.54 billion years.
* Rotation: About 23.9 hours.
* Revolution: About 365.25 days (1 year).

Mars:

* Size: Mean radius of about 3,390 kilometers.
* Orbit: Average orbital radius of about 228 10^6 kilometers (1.52 AU).
* Age: Approximately 4.5 billion years.
* Rotation: About 24.6 hours.
* Revolution: About 687 Earth days (1.9 Earth years).

Jupiter:

* Size: Equatorial radius of about 71,492 kilometers.
* Orbit: Average orbital radius of about 778.5 10^6 kilometers (5.20 AU).
* Age: Approximately 4.5 billion years.
* Rotation: About 9.9 hours.
* Revolution: About 11.9 Earth years.

Saturn:

* Size: Equatorial radius of about 60,268 kilometers.
* Orbit: Average orbital radius of about 1.43 10^9 kilometers (9.54 AU).
* Age: Approximately 4.5 billion years.
* Rotation: About 10.7 hours.
* Revolution: About 29.5 Earth years.

Uranus:

* Size: Equatorial radius of about 25,559 kilometers.
* Orbit: Average orbital radius of about 2.87 10^9 kilometers (19.22 AU).
* Age: Approximately 4.5 billion years.
* Rotation: About 17.2 hours (retrograde rotation on a tilted axis).
* Revolution: About 84 Earth years.

Neptune:

* Size: Equatorial radius of about 24,764 kilometers.
* Orbit: Average orbital radius of about 4.5 10^9 kilometers (30.06 AU).
* Age: Approximately 4.5 billion years.
* Rotation: About 16.1 hours.
* Revolution: About 165 Earth years.

Pluto (Dwarf Planet):

* Size: Mean radius of about 1,188 kilometers.
* Orbit: Average orbital radius of about 5.9 10^9 kilometers (39.5 AU), with a highly elliptical and inclined orbit.
* Age: Approximately 4.5 billion years.
* Rotation: About 6.4 Earth days (retrograde).
* Revolution: About 248 Earth years.

Space:

* Size: Essentially infinite and continuously expanding. The observable universe has a diameter of about 8.8 10^26 meters (93 billion light-years).
* Orbit: Does not orbit a single object. Structures within space (stars, galaxies, etc.) have their own orbits within larger systems.
* Age: The universe is estimated to be about 13.8 billion years old.
* Rotation/Revolution: Space itself doesn't rotate or revolve as a single entity. However, the galaxies and other structures within it do rotate and move relative to each other due to gravity and the expansion of the universe.
